Search Engine Optimisation

Have your website appear on search engines like Google, Google Maps and Bing

Having a website alone is not enough these days. It's vital to stay at the top of any search page when a potential customer searches for your industry in your location. Using a variety of strategies we help push your business to the top of the page whenever your next potential customer searches for your sevices.

What we offer:

  • Local Search Engine Optimisation: Our strategies focus on localisation of your business in order to get noticed in your exact area. We inform Google of your business location, the services you offer and all other relevant information. By aligning your website and its metadata with your Google business account we ensure that Google understands your relevancy in your local area.
  • Keyword Ranking Research: Within every industry and location there is a particular set of keywords which your potential customers search for. We do the research for your particular business keywords and implement them into your website to help you bring in more customers.
  • Client Consultation: We offer our clients advice on what they can do themselves to improve their search engine rankings and how to maintain their status once achieved.
